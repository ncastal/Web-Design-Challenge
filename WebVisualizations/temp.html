Latitude Vs. Temperature

As expected, the temperature changes with latitude. The closer to the equator (0 Deg Latitude), the higher the temperature.
However, the southern hemisphere also appears to be warmer than the northern hemisphere. This may be a result of when the data was gathered which was in January of 2020. The northern hemisphere would be expierencing winter while the sourthern hemisphere would be expierencing summer.
